About the Elegant Eta Beta Chapter

The Elegant Eta Beta Chapter of Alpha Kappa Alpha Sorority, Inc. was chartered on November 11, 1972 by a group of 13 sophisticated young women from Vanderbilt University and George Peabody College for Teachers. The establishment of our chapter by these dynamic women, made Alpha Kappa Alpha Sorority, Inc. the FIRST Black Greek letter sorority at Vanderbilt University. About HeartbreAKA

In accordance with our current initiatives, the Elegant Eta Beta Chapter of Alpha Kappa Alpha Sorority Inc. hosts HeartbreAKA every year to raise money for the Mary Parrish Center. HeartbreAKA is a service auction where attendees will bid on the individual expertise and aid of eligible Vanderbilt bachelors to be used during a virtual Escape Room experience!

Do you want to attend this event? Login to your Google account and begin donating supplies and money to get AKA dollars that you can use at the event.
